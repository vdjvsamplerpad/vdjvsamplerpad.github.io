Add to Home Screen on iPhone or iPad

You are already on the correct page. Follow these Safari steps.

Just tap then "Add to Home Screen"
  1. Tap the Share button in the Safari toolbar.
  2. Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen".
  3. Confirm the app name and tap Add.
  4. Launch the new icon from Home Screen. The installed app opens at vdjvsamplerpad.online/.
If "Add to Home Screen" is missing, switch to Safari (not in-app browser), then refresh this page.
